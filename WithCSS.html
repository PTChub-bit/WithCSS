Call of Duty: Mobile

Call of Duty: Mobile is a free-to-play first-person shooter game released in 2019. It brings console-style gameplay to mobile devices with high-quality graphics and exciting combat.

Game Overview

The game includes both multiplayer and battle royale modes. Players can customize loadouts, weapons, and skills while competing in different maps and missions. [oai_citation:1‡GamesRadar+](https://www.gamesradar.com/call-of-duty-mobile-guide/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

Game Modes

  • Team Deathmatch – teams fight to reach the kill limit
  • Frontline – players respawn at base and earn points by kills
  • Domination – capture zones to earn points
  • Search and Destroy – strategic mode with no respawn
  • Battle Royale – last player or team survives

How to Play

Players use touch controls to move, aim, and shoot. Each match has objectives like eliminating enemies or capturing zones. Strategy and teamwork are important to win.

Scoring System

Points are earned by kills, capturing objectives, or completing missions. For example, teams win by reaching a target score like 50 kills in Team Deathmatch. Main Characters

  • Captain Price – leader and tactical expert
  • Ghost – stealth specialist with a skull mask

Enemies

Enemies can be real players in multiplayer matches or opposing squads in battle royale mode. Players must eliminate them to win.
